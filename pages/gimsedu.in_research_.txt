Research at GIMS
At GIMS, theCenter for Research and Faculty Development (CRFD)serves as the driving force behind our academic and scholarly excellence. This dedicated cell works tirelessly to nurture a vibrant research ecosystem and empower both faculty and students in their pursuit of knowledge, innovation, and impactful contributions.
At GIMS, research is more than an academic pursuit; it is the cornerstone of our commitment to innovation and societal impact. We foster a vibrant ecosystem that empowers our faculty and students to explore, collaborate, and contribute meaningful solutions to real-world challenges. Our approach is built on three key pillars:
Our commitment to academic excellence is driven by the scholarly pursuits of our faculty. We are proud to have created an environment where high-level research thrives. This dedication is reflected in our numbers:
A dynamic research culture is fueled by continuous learning. GIMS ensures our faculty and students remain at the forefront of their fields through a rich calendar of development programs.
We believe the most powerful solutions emerge at the intersection of disciplines. GIMS actively champions a culture of collaboration, breaking down traditional silos between Management, Computer Applications, and Commerce.
Our research culture extends directly to our students. Through initiatives like theBusiness Case Circuit Series,BCA/MCA Project Expos, andfaculty-mentored projects, students gain invaluable hands-on experience, learning to apply theoretical knowledge and develop the analytical skills needed to thrive.
