Cultural activities make a campus very vibrant and GIMS gets its energy from various cultural activities. May it be singing, dancing, acting, poetry, drawing GIMS ions live their life to the fullest in these cultural extravaganza. All these activities are coordinated through respective clubs such as Kalanirmithi. Enthusiasts can join these clubs through proper Auditions.. Students will get a great exposure being in these clubs and emerge as competent team players.
The Fine Arts Club of Global Institute of Management Sciences is named asKALA NIRMITI. It acts as a roof under which all the students with different cultural interests come together.The main objective of Kalanirmiti is to encourage and promote art and cultural aspects among the students, to build a healthy organization spirit and give them an opportunity to exhibit their cultural talent and actively take part in the artistic events conducted by the club. The club also takes the responsibility of organizing various cultural events throughout the year and facilitates students to take charge of managing the events. The club is concerned in promoting and motivating the innovative ideas, leadership and decision-making skills of the students in a positive way.
