Research at GIMS
At GIMS, the Center for Research and Faculty Development (CRFD) serves as the driving force behind our academic and scholarly excellence. This dedicated cell works tirelessly to nurture a vibrant research ecosystem and empower both faculty and students in their pursuit of knowledge, innovation, and impactful contributions.

At GIMS, research is more than an academic pursuit; it is the cornerstone of our commitment to innovation and societal impact. We foster a vibrant ecosystem that empowers our faculty and students to explore, collaborate, and contribute meaningful solutions to real-world challenges. Our approach is built on three key pillars:

Faculty Empowerment & Scholarly Growth
Our commitment to academic excellence is driven by the scholarly pursuits of our faculty. We are proud to have created an environment where high-level research thrives. This dedication is reflected in our numbers:

Over 34% of our faculty are either Ph.D. holders or are actively pursuing their doctorate.
This includes a strong core of established Ph.D. holders who lead our research initiatives and mentor junior faculty.
An additional 15% of our faculty are currently enrolled in Ph.D. programs, with the full support and encouragement of the institution, ensuring our research culture is constantly growing.
Continuous Skill Development
A dynamic research culture is fueled by continuous learning. GIMS ensures our faculty and students remain at the forefront of their fields through a rich calendar of development programs.

Monthly In-House FDPs: Regular sessions focused on emerging research methodologies, pedagogical techniques, and academic writing.
Intensive Multi-Day FDPs: We organize deep-dive, multi-day Faculty Development Programmes on critical themes. Flagship initiatives like our 5-Day FDP on “New-Age Research Tools Using AI” and “Adapting the Digital Landscape” provide immersive learning experiences.
Expert-Led Workshops & Talks: Our calendar is filled with specialized training and insightful talks from industry and academic leaders on cutting-edge topics, including Data Visualization with JMP and Power BI, modern statistical methods, and many more.
Interdisciplinary Collaboration & Student Involvement
We believe the most powerful solutions emerge at the intersection of disciplines. GIMS actively champions a culture of collaboration, breaking down traditional silos between Management, Computer Applications, and Commerce.

Our research culture extends directly to our students. Through initiatives like the Business Case Circuit Series, BCA/MCA Project Expos, and faculty-mentored projects, students gain invaluable hands-on experience, learning to apply theoretical knowledge and develop the analytical skills needed to thrive.
