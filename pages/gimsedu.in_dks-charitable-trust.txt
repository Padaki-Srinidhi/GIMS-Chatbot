DKS Charitable Institute (R)
Over the past 24 years, Global Institute of Management Sciences (GIMS), have been able to create an identity of its own and able to place our students in successful positions to have a satisfying and rewarding career. We create challenging environment to work and improve their productivity. We impart quality education to cater to the evolving needs of the students, industries, and the community.

DKS Charitable Institute (R) founded in the year 1999. Under the Chairmanship of Karnataka’s dynamic leader and educationist Sri D. K. Shivakumar, the trust has emerged with a chain of educational institutions. His visionary values are unparalleled in the education field.
