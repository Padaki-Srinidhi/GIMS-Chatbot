What are the rules surrounding attendance requirement?
You must attain at least 75% overall attendance for classes, inclusive of all approved leave of absence, failing which you will be debarred from taking the examination/assessments. If you need to apply for leave of absence, please submit to the respective HOD’s well in advance for consideration.
Will I be given a student ID card?
All GIMS students will be issued with a student ID card.
