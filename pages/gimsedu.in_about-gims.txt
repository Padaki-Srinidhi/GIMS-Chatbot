About GIMS
Global Institute of Management Sciences (GIMS) is a NAAC Accredited, AICTE Approved, and Bangalore University Affiliated institution, committed to delivering quality education at all levels. An ambitious initiative of the D.K.S. Charitable Institute®, GIMS functions under the visionary leadership of Sri D. K. Shivakumar, Honourable Deputy Chief Minister of Karnataka. The institution operates with the guiding motto: “Quality education for all at all levels.”

Nestled in the serene and green surroundings of Rajarajeshwari Nagar, in southeastern Bengaluru, GIMS is recognized by the Government of Karnataka, approved by the All India Council for Technical Education (AICTE), New Delhi, and enjoys academic recognition under Section 2(f) of the UGC Act, 1956.

GIMS proudly hosts two premier Centres of Excellence (COEs):

CISCO Networking Academy
Harvard Business Publishing Education COE, which enhances academic rigor and global relevance.
