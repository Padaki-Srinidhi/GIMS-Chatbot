Computer Lab
Overview
At GIMS each student is exposed to newer ways of learning, exploring and researching. The Computer Lab designed with the latest of personal computers, gadgets etc. To help the students in dedicating time to technology and search for their information from the World Wide Web.
This is the Internet era which is connecting millions of people to each other. For a holistic development of the student, it is best to provide them access to the vast knowledge bank of the world for them to learn and progress. The Computer Lab is diligently planned, equipped and maintained to provide the students with latest of technology, software versions and programs to know the computer world better. This shall bring them closer to practical knowledge of Technological Science.
Students, faculty and staff have access to the computer labs, which provide the tools and technologies to produce websites, edit papers, complete class assignments, communicate via email, conduct data analysis. Microsoft Windows software is available for word processing, statistics, spreadsheets and database management. A variety of graphics and website-creation software programs are also available.
The college has established a high-speed campus-wide network that connects all its departments consisting of 155 systems. A fully distributed computing environment based on clusters of workstations and PCs provides the users ready access to computing resources, services software and applications. The environment is tailored to the specific teaching/learning needs of each department.
The college has Internet Facility with two fibre connections one from BSNL with 200Mbps and another from ACT with 500Mbps.
