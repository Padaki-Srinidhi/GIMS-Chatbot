Overview
The inception of the Business lab in the institution is to develop practical applicability of subject in global competence. The main intention of Business Lab is to inculcate knowledge, and need-based work which caters to the student’s requirements. The intention is to enhance and enrich the skills and entrepreneurship abilities of the students.

Charts, models, specimen copies, and magazines are displayed in the lab for the benefit of the students. Business lab is also used for interaction with peers, presentations, competitions were conducted. Through lab, we also facilitated the students to do the mini-projects, and paper presentations, and also made an effort to build a research attitude in the minds of students.
