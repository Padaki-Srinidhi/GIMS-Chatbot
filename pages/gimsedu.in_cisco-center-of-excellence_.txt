Introduction:
The Virtual Internship Program (VIP) 2024 is a collaborative initiative between Cisco and the All India Council for Technical Education (AICTE), designed to empower students in Cyber Security and align with India’s digital skilling agenda.
Objectives
– Skill Development: Equip students with essential technical skills through Cisco Networking Academy courses.
– Industry Engagement: Connect students with cybersecurity professionals to explore career pathways.
– Mentorship: Provide guidance from accredited instructors and local mentors.

Program Structure
– Eligibility: BCA, MCA, and other technical degree students in 1st, 2nd, and 3rd years.
– Focus Areas: Cyber Security principles and Packet Tracer simulation.
– Duration: May 2024 to July 2024

Benefits
– Acquire recognized technical skills and certifications from Cisco.
– Engage with industry experts for insights into job opportunities.
– Enhance employability with valuable certificates for resumes.
