Overview
Department of Commerce and Management, a dynamic hub where academic rigor meets real-world application. We offer comprehensive undergraduate (B.Com, BBA) and postgraduate (MBA) programs designed not just to educate, but to inspire and transform students into confident, ethical, and industry-ready professionals. Our mission is to cultivate the strategic minds and resilient leaders who will drive the future of business.

Our approach is built on a powerful, three-pronged strategy:

An Industry-Integrated & Experiential Curriculum
We believe that true learning happens by doing. Our curriculum moves far beyond traditional lectures to immerse students in the practical challenges of the business world. Through a multifaceted approach, you will:

Solve Real-World Problems in our dynamic Business Case Circuit Series.
Gain Direct Industry Exposure through industrial visits, corporate expert talks, and collaborations with leading firms like ProSculpt, Talisma, and Kotak Life.
Master In-Demand Skills in hands-on workshops covering everything from Emotional Intelligence and Data Visualization to Vedic Mathematics and Corporate Readiness.
Pathways to Global Success
We provide our students with a distinct competitive advantage through globally recognized qualifications and strategic partnerships. Our collaborations include:

Harvard Business Publishing Education: Offering a world-class learning experience with access to renowned case studies and simulations.
ACCA Qualification: A direct pathway to a globally respected professional accounting credential through our partnership with the Global Financial Training Institute (GFTI).
1-on-1 Industry Mentorship: Our unique program with Mentor Together pairs every student with a dedicated professional mentor to guide their career journey.
Guidance from Accomplished Faculty & Thought Leaders
Our department is led by a team of highly qualified and experienced faculty, many of whom are published authors in national and international journals. Under the award-winning leadership of our Director, recognized as an “Inspiring Education Hero,” our faculty are committed to academic excellence and student success. They not only teach but actively mentor, fostering a culture of curiosity, critical thinking, and social responsibility that prepares students to lead with integrity
